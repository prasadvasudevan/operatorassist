why it matters
Making the right financial choices is important to your financial future. By modeling your finances, you can better prepare for opportunities and economic storms. Forecast finances out five or fifteen years and see how to change the future to your favor! Confidently make informed decisions to set you on the right path to achieve your financial- and life-goals.

Personal Economics modeling tool is becoming increasingly valuable and relevant to consumers of all ages and stages in life. It allows for a quick and simple way to visualize future economic circumstances based on a combination of factors within a person's control and outside a person's control. As the saying goes, "knowledge is power" and Personal Economics puts economic decision-making knowledge and power into the hands of ordinary consumers, giving them the ability to take control of their financial future despite the sometimes tumultuous economic climate in the world around us.
The Possibilities Are Endless
You can model the financial effects of key decisions, and the compare these scenarios against your current situation and your future financial goals. Model scenarios such as:
  • Refinancing your home
  • Changing your investments
  • Going to College
  • Buying a car
  • Marriage/Divorce/Partners
  • Home Ownership
  • Natural Disasters and Unexpected Events
  • Starting/Losing a job
  • Starting/Losing a Business
  • Planning for retirement/retiring
  • Death of a Family Members
The current version of the tool uses standard scenarios. However, we will shortly release the full version which allows for nearly limitless combinations of “what if” scenarios.

Contact us to be notified as new features are added.
Managing Risk and Ensuring Financial Success
Managing financial risk in today's environment can be overwhelming to even those with extensive financial training. Future View gives both the novice and the expert a set of powerful tools to model the effects of both planned and unplanned events. What would happen to my financial plans if inflation goes up suddenly? Is there a realistic timeframe when I can plan to retire? Would the cost of another degree be worthwhile over the long run? These questions and many more can now be modeled and compared with this powerful new financial modeling tool.
