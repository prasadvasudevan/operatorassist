about us
FrontPaw Solutions, LLC began with  a simple goal to provide economic modeling tools to individual consumers, enabling them to make informed economic decisions. With the rapidly changing economic picture in the world, many consumers have no real way to keep up with the many variables that are changing daily - investment returns, interest rates, home prices, retirement account balances, and many others. Personal Economics Modeling provides a fun and easy way to “see” into your financial future.
