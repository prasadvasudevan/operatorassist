Data Sources
future view™ provides the ability to compare your individual financial circumstances to those of a national sample of households. This comparison is provided in the "Benchmark" service offering. The Benchmark numbers are derived from a variety of government data sources as indicated below. These numbers are subject to sampling errors, reliability limitations and other factors that may make them unsuitable for application in a specific situation or scenario. See the following notes and the source data descriptions for these specific databases for additional details. .
  • Per-Household Asset and Liability numbers are derived from aggregate numbers in the Federal Reserve Flow of Funds report (referenced below). These aggregate numbers are divided by the number of households as indicated on the Census Bureau to get an average per-household number. These numbers are only an approximation as the aggregate numbers include assets and liabilities of non-profit organizations of which there are over a million.
  • National Benchmark "Income" is taken from the Bureau of the Census, Current Populations Survey (CPS) for 2010, median income for all households ($49,445 per year)
